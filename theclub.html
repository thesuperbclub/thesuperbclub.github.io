The Superb Club is a mythical organization emerging from a chance encounter between founders Lighting Bolt and Inorganic Material in a volcanic bar near Lisbon. Originally known as the Quarrymen, the organization emerged a few billion years later as the Superb Club after learning how to play their instruments and developing a brain capable of (limited) self-awareness.

You may notice that that a particularly pleasing regular quadrilateral shares both the same first letter and same number of letters as "superb". This, of course, is no coincidence! The most sacred of shapes, The Wholly Square, is intricately tied to the club's identity via these two powerful, shared features.
