    Hello, this is a Superb Square, This type of square is used to increase the amount of Superbness in its immediate vicinity. Other squares may be manufactured which alternative effects. For example, you may in your travels encounter a Spooky Square or maybe even (if you are unlucky) a Sordid Square. The powers granted by such squares, which may be identified by their distinctive alliterative and hexanumeric quality, are universal and irresistible. Likewise, they are also wholly imaginary. Please be careful when administering to children under the age of 12.
