    Hello, this is a Superb Square. This type of square is used to increase the amount of Superbness in its immediate vicinity. Other squares may be manufactured which alternative effects. For example, you may in your travels encounter a Spooky Square or maybe even (if you are unlucky) a Sordid Square. The powers granted by such squares, which may be identified by their distinctive alliterative and hexagrammatic quality, are universal and irresistible. Please exercise caution and do not operate heavy machine while under the influence.
